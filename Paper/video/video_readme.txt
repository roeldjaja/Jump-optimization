"Explosive Motions with Compliant Actuation Arrangements in Articulated Robots"

Roel Djajadiningrat, Wesley Roozing*, and Nikos G. Tsagarakis
*Corresponding author

The video attachment "2018_jump_optimisation_video.mp4" contains the following:
- Summary of what is presented in this work;
- Videos showing the initial guess and optimised motions for all actuation configurations;
- Highlights of the most important results.

Multimedia requirements:
The video attachment is encoded with the H.264 codec without an audio track, in the mp4 container. There are no particular requirements for playing the video: it should run on any modern video player.
